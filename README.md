## 🚀 Overview

ClinicFlow is a digital healthcare platform built to help clinics manage daily operations efficiently.

The platform enables doctors, staff, and patients to interact seamlessly through appointment scheduling, digital records, prescriptions, billing systems, and healthcare analytics.

---

## ❌ Problems Solved

Traditional clinics often face:

- Long patient queues
- Manual appointment scheduling
- Paper-based medical records
- Prescription management issues
- Billing inefficiencies
- Lack of patient communication

---

## ✅ Solution

ClinicFlow digitizes clinic operations by providing:

- Online appointment booking
- Electronic medical records
- Digital prescriptions
- Automated billing
- Patient management
- Healthcare analytics
